BLUE16 is a free, community-driven platform for hosting and playing classic Roblox games and experiences. Our mission is to preserve the legacy of old Roblox clients and make them accessible to everyone. With BLUE16, you can:
  • Connect and play solo or with friends on every Roblox client that exists
  • Host your own servers or join public ones, as well official ones by BLUE16
  • Have no limits to anything
  • Enjoy a secure-friendly experience
  • Contribute to an open-source project and help shape its future


BLUE16 is built by fans, for fans. Whether you're here to relive nostalgia, learn about Roblox history, or just have fun, you're welcome in our community. Join us in keeping the spirit of classic Roblox alive!
