BLUE16 is a free, community-driven platform for hosting and playing classic Roblox games and experiences. Our mission is to preserve the legacy of old Roblox clients and make them accessible to everyone.

Play Classic Games

Experience the nostalgia of early Roblox with access to classic clients and games from 2006-2024.

Host Your Own Server

Create your own private server or join public ones to play with friends.

Places Library

Browse and download thousands of classic Roblox places to play in your own server.
